Global Third-Party Partners

To support our free mission of providing accurate Zakat tools, Hisabify collaborates with world-class partners. These entities may set cookies on your browser through our site:

  • Google AdSense: Hisabify uses the DoubleClick DART cookie to serve ads based on your visit to our site and other financial platforms. This ensures the ads you see are relevant and high-quality.
  • Google Analytics: Helps Hisabify analyze global traffic flow to ensure our servers remain fast and responsive for users in every time zone.
  • Infrastructure CDNs: Used to load our elegant fonts and complex mathematical scripts instantly, ensuring Hisabify performs flawlessly on any device.

Ethical Data Usage

In accordance with Islamic principles of Amanah (Trust), Hisabify does not sell your private calculation data to third parties. Our cookies are strictly used to improve the interface and allow us to continue providing these services without cost to the user.

Whether you are calculating Zakat on Gold, Silver, Savings, or Business Assets, Hisabify ensures that your interaction remains private and your browser settings are respected at all times.

Your Authority & Control

As a valued user of Hisabify, you hold full authority over your digital footprint. You can choose to:

  1. Browser Settings: Adjust your Chrome, Safari, or Firefox settings to block all cookies from Hisabify.
  2. Opt-Out of Tracking: Use Google's official opt-out tools to prevent your data from being used by Analytics or AdSense.
  3. Clear Session Data: Manually delete cookies after finishing your Zakat calculation for the year.
