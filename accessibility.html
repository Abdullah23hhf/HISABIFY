Universal Accessibility

Our commitment to ensuring that Zakat calculation and financial literacy are accessible to every member of the global Ummah, regardless of ability.

Compliance Level: WCAG 2.1 AA

Al-Hisab believes that access to spiritual financial tools is a fundamental right. We are dedicated to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We are actively working to increase the accessibility and usability of our website and in doing so adhere to many of the available standards and guidelines.

Accessibility Features

Full Keyboard Access

Optimized for users who navigate without a mouse. Every calculator input and menu item is accessible via 'Tab' navigation.

Visual Contrast

We use high-contrast ratios for text and UI elements to assist users with low vision or color blindness.

Screen Reader Ready

Our code follows semantic HTML structures (WAI-ARIA labels) to provide a clear experience for assistive voice technologies.

Technical Conformance

We leverage the latest web technologies to provide a robust experience across all platforms. Our site is tested against the following specifications:

Technology Application
HTML5 / WAI-ARIA Semantic structure and accessibility labels.
CSS3 / Flexbox Responsive and scalable layout design.
ECMAScript 6 Reliable calculator logic and processing.

Ongoing Improvements

Accessibility is an ongoing journey. As web standards evolve, Al-Hisab undergoes periodic manual and automated audits to identify and rectify potential barriers. Our goal is to meet and exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 standards.
